About US

SOUL KITCHEN

Tenebris tattoo parlor was founded 10 years ago by Cash Mat - a self-
taught tattoo enthusiast from the great city of Chicago. Tenebris has
made its mark as Chicago's renowned tattoo destination. Our main
goal is to turn your inspiration into a fine work of art and craft the
best possible tattoos. Tenebris has become a hub of tattoo artists and
aficionados from all walks of life, disciplines and ages.
